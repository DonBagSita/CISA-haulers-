Who We Are

The Corridor Specialists

CISA Hauliers Limited is Kenya's trusted name in heavy haulage, crane hire, and project logistics — operating across the critical Mombasa–Nairobi–Uganda Corridor and beyond. From the port's edge to your project site, we move what others won't.

With a fleet of low-bed trailers, flatbeds, and crane-equipped rigs, we handle oversized machinery, industrial equipment, oil & gas infrastructure, and bulk cargo with precision and care. Our operators are certified, experienced, and safety-first — every load, every time.
